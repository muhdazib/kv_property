About KVProperty

Clearer property insight

KVProperty brings together Klang Valley's residential transaction records, infrastructure locations and surrounding amenities into one readable platform. Instead of looking at price data on its own, users can understand how each area is supported by transport access, commercial activity, education hubs and district-level market movement.

The goal is to make property analysis easier to compare and explain. Users can review market trends in the dashboard, explore location context on the map and use the prediction workspace as a supporting tool for residential price estimation.
